What is StrokeSense?

StrokeSense is a web-based AI game that helps learners practice and master Baybayin handwriting through fun, interactive exercises. With instant feedback, gamified challenges, and progress tracking, StrokeSense makes learning Baybayin easier, faster, and more engaging. By combining modern technology with cultural heritage, StrokeSense empowers users to preserve Filipino history while improving their handwriting skills.
